About
DJ Murchison

Hi! My name is DJ, I'm from San Diego, California, and a third year transfer at UC Berkeley. I love to travel, surf, hike, and of course, take pictures. I am currently studying geography with an emphasis in Geographic Information Systems (GIS).

I began getting into photography a few years ago when I travelled down to Chile (Futaleufú seen left) and Peru on a gap year trip with my little GoPRO. I would take pictures of primarily the landscape, but also take a few of people, architecture, and other things I found interesting. When I reterned home, I began taking a few pictures around San Diego and other trips or adventures I would find myself on.

Now that I am attending Cal, I've become a lot more interested in teaching myself how to use and operate a camera, including adjusting it manually. I took GEOG 189 a visual geography class with Joel Wanek, infusing my interests of geography and photography. I can't wait to learn more about photography and the opportunity to share thos photos!
